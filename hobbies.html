Trail Running

Why I run

I love the directness of running: the work is honest, the feedback is immediate, and progress is earned slowly enough that it means something. There is deep satisfaction in seeing dedication become capacity, then getting to spend that capacity on big terrain, mountain views, and the reward of finishing what I started.
