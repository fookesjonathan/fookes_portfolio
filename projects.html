Canyons Route Planning

This project highlights route research and field planning for canyon objectives. The goal is to move beyond a simple GPS line and build a practical route brief: approach notes, technical sections, weather windows, water expectations, bailout options, and the places where the plan needs a fresh decision.

What it captures

The route plan organizes beta into a field-ready format: known anchors, potential hazards, group pace assumptions, alternate exits, and uncertainty that deserves attention before committing.

Why it matters

Good planning makes room for good judgment. It keeps the team focused on the terrain in front of them while preserving enough structure to make better decisions when the day changes.
